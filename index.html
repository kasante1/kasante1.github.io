Projects

  • Constitution of Ghana, 1992 Plus

    check web version here.

    This app is a simple but reliable reference for an accurate and current posture of any provision in the Constitution of the Republic of Ghana, 1992. Interpretation is an essential feature of Ghana’s legal system. It provides certainty to some highly contentious aspects of the 1992 Constitution. This app was made to make the 1992 constitution and its interpreted provisions available to the public. This is an android app on the Google Play Store. I used Flutter + Dart.



  • Arguments, Issues, Holdings Extractor

    This project automatically extracts Arguments, Issues and Holdings from law cases. I made this with Python.



  • The Penalty Unit Calculator

    It provides the monetary equivalent of a penalty unit. A “Penalty unit” refers to such units established by Section 26 of the Interpretation Act, 2009 (Act 792).
